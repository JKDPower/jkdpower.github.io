Your first point of contact for training with our group should be Jeremy Lynch. He is the primary instructor at the WNG headquarters in Redlands, California. Please [email Sifu Jeremy](mailto:jkdjeremy@gmail.com) and provide your name, contact info, and a brief description of your martial arts background. Beginners are also welcome at our classes, so if you haven't trained before that's OK too.

If for any reason you can't get in touch with Sifu Jeremy, please [contact Mike Blesch](mailto:mike@jkdwednite.com) by email and let him know what you need. Mike is also the primary contact for any questions related to this website.

Business matters, seminar requests and all other inquiries should be directed to <contact@jkdwednite.com>.

If you are trying to find a qualified JKD instructor near you, start with our [Official](/official/) and [Affiliated](/affiliated/) Instructor lists.
